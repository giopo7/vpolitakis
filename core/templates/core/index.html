Since 1990, Vassilis Politakis
has worked to understand and recreate
the pottery of the Aegean and Cypriot bronze age.

His expertise is supported by collaborations with:

The British School at Athens (BSA) The Italian arch. school The American Study Center of Aegean Prehistory (INSTAP-EC) The Heraklion Archaeological Museum The Official Hellenic Institute of Material Science (Demokritos) The University of Crete The Greek Ministry of Culture And several individual academic archaeologists
